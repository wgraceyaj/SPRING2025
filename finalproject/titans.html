About the Titans

Each Subject of Ymir (Eldians) has the ability to become a Pure Titan. This is different from an Intelligent Titan because Pure Titans are mindless and their only purpose is to eat humans. Pure Titans stay as a titan forever until they pass. Intelligent Titans possess unique powers that are specific to that Titan they possessed. Intelligent Titans are able to go back into their human form.

The original Founder Titan, Ymir, only lived for 13 years as a Titan so those that posses a Intelligent Titan have a lifespan of 13 years before they have to find an heir to their Titan otherwise their Titan will be passed along to someone random that is a Subject of Ymir. The proccess to become a Intelligent Titan is the Subject of Ymir has to become a Pure Titan which if from a intake of a Titans spinal fluid, then eating someone in their Pure Titan form that possess an Intelligent Titan.

Most Pure Titans aren't active at night because they rely on the sunlight for energy.

Those that inherit Intelligent Titans are able to see the memories of past Titans.

Founding Titan

  • Able to turn Subjects of Ymir into Titans
  • Alter memories and control minds of Titans and Subjects of Ymir
  • Only royal blood is able to unlock the true power of the Founding Titan

Armored Titan

  • Has armor like skin
  • Has the ability to harden areas of the body to protect itself

Attack Titan

  • Able to receive memories from all inheritors from the past and future of the Attack Titan
  • Always fighting for Freedom and doesn't follow the Founding Titan's will

Beast Titan

  • Able to form as different animals and take their physical characteristics. This changes between its inheritors
  • Zeke the last Beast Titan took the form of a monkey and the person before him was a sheep

Cart Titan

  • Walks on all fours
  • Has great endurance which allows them to stay in their form for long periods of time
  • Able to transfrom more than 3 times

Colossus Titan

  • Able to release an immense amount of steam and blast it like a furnace
  • Uses a lot of energy which limits a users abilty to transform a lot
  • Dangerous to its surrounding when transformation happens because a explosion happens

Female Titan

  • Able to harden areas of its skin
  • Able to summon Pure Titans with its scream

Jaw Titan

  • Has sharp teeth and claws
  • Able to break/damage almost anything

War Hammer Titan

  • Able to create all kinds of weapons from its own harden flesh
  • Able to hide the user away from the Titan body within a crystal casing but still be in controll their Titan fully

Doomsday Titan

  • A Titan that technically isn't part of the 9 Intelligent Titans but was formed because of the last user of the Founding and Attack Titan
  • Controller of the Rumbling
  • Woke up the Titans in the walls
  • Destroyed 80% of humanity
  • The last Titan and the one that got rid of all Titans that existed
  • Reverted all Pure Titans back into humans after its passing

Ackermans

The Ackermans are like Titans but in human form. They aren't able to regenerate like Titans but has a supernatural strength. They used to be the guards of the royal family because of how strong they are. Because they aren't Eldians, their memories can't be munipulated and they aren't able to turn into Titans. Since the King isn't able to control them, he feared them and ended up bring Ackermans to the brink of extinction which is why those that are Ackermans don't know why they are so strong and their background.
